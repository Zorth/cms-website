Casino Royale without weaponry involved. The stakes are high. Can you win the night?

Well known casino games are brought to your doorstep. Without an entry fee or monetary value to the poker chips, playing is done stress free. The Dragons can explain any of the games when needed.

## General Info:

* You don’t have to be there from the start.
* Formal attire is appreciated, but not required.
* Our event is legal since the chips don’t have value.
* Questions? Feel free to ask the Dragons.

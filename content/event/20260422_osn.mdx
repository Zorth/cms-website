Ever had that itch to play some Table Top RPGs, but never had the time or maybe it seems a little too daunting?
Well look no further! Tarragon is once again hosting some one-shot sessions.
A **one-shot is** a moment where every aspect of an entire TTRPG Campaign gets reduced to a single session of around **2-3 hours.**

If That interested you even slightly why not just join us, and help us make this night even more special.

Our “system” that we will be running will be either Dungeons & Dragons or Pathfinder. If you don't know what this means then sign up for what sounds cooler, they don't differ that much.

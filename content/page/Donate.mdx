# Support Tarragon

Tarragon is a non-profit organization (VZW) dedicated to bringing people together through TTRPGs, board games, and other geeky activities. Our mission is to provide a welcoming space for everyone to explore their hobbies and connect with others.

Your donations help us:
- Maintain and expand our collection of board games and RPG books.
- Organize special events and workshops.
- Keep our weekly meetups accessible and free for everyone.
- Support the local geek community in Kortrijk.

Every contribution, big or small, makes a difference!

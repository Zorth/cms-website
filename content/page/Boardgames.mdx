# Boardgames & Strategic Games in Kortrijk

Tarragon is the home of **Boardgames in Kortrijk**. Every Wednesday, we gather at **Het Textielhuis** to play everything from the latest Eurogames and heavy strategy games to lighthearted party games and classics.

## What to Expect?
Our community is full of boardgame enthusiasts who are always happy to teach new games. You don't need to bring anything; we have a growing library, and members often bring their own latest acquisitions.

*   **Strategic Eurogames:** For those who love resource management and long-term planning.
*   **Ameritrash & Thematic Games:** Dive into deep stories and high-stakes dice rolls.
*   **Party & Social Games:** Quick, fun games that are perfect for meeting new people.
*   **Warhammer & Skirmish Games:** We have space for tactical miniature combat.

## Weekly Boardgame Night
*   **When:** Every Wednesday, 19:00 - 22:00
*   **Where:** Het Textielhuis, Rijselsestraat 19, 8500 Kortrijk
*   **Price:** Free entry for everyone!

## Looking for "Bordspellen Kortrijk"?
Whether you are a seasoned veteran or just looking for a new hobby, Tarragon provides a welcoming environment to play **boardgames in Kortrijk**. Our "Boardgame Night" (BGN) is the perfect way to spend your Wednesday evening.

# TTRPG & Geek Events in Kortrijk

Tarragon offers professional **Gamemaster (GM) services** and unique geeky events for every occasion. Whether you're a seasoned player or have never touched a 20-sided die, we ensure an unforgettable experience.

## Gamemaster Services
Our experienced storytellers guide you through the most popular Tabletop RPGs (TTRPGs). We provide everything: dice, character sheets, and an immersive story.

*   **Dungeons & Dragons (D&D):** The world's most famous role-playing game. Perfect for beginners and advanced players alike.
*   **Pathfinder:** For those who love depth and tactical combat.
*   **Call of Cthulhu:** Dive into cosmic horror and mystery in the 1920s.
*   **Into the Odd:** Fast, strange, and extremely accessible.
*   **And more:** We support various systems based on your preferences.

## Team-building & Corporate Events
Strengthen the bond between colleagues by solving problems together in a fantasy setting. TTRPGs are the ultimate exercise in communication and creativity.

## Birthdays & Bachelor Parties
Celebrate your special day with an epic adventure. We tailor the scenario to the group, whether it's a lighthearted dungeon crawl or a thrilling murder mystery.

## Quizzes, LARP & More
In addition to role-playing, we also organize:
*   **Geek-themed Quizzes:** Test your knowledge of movies, series, games, and more.
*   **LARP (Live Action Role-Playing):** Literally become your character in an interactive setting.
*   **Workshops:** Learn how to become a GM yourself or how to paint miniatures.

## 📍 Region Kortrijk
We are proud residents of **Kortrijk** and offer our services throughout the region. Looking for **"D&D Kortrijk"**, **"LARP Kortrijk"**, or a unique outing in West Flanders? Tarragon is the place to be.

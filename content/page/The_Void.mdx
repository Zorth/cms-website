# The Void: A Living World Campaign

Want to play TTRPGs but can't commit to a fixed weekly group? **The Void** is our persistent, "West Marches" style campaign where you choose when to play.

Whether you're a fan of **Dungeons & Dragons 5e** or looking to try the deep customization of **Pathfinder 2e**, you'll find a seat at our table. Our world is home to both systems, giving you the freedom to explore the same lore through different rules.

## 🚀 Quick Start
1. **Join the Tavern**: Hop into the [**#ouroboros_inn**](https://discord.com/channels/878674783972261918/1322273585032724510) on Discord to meet the community.
2. **Find a Session**: Browse upcoming games for both D&D and Pathfinder on the [**Guild Planning Tool**](https://guild.tarragon.be).
3. **Read the Lore**: Check the [**Void Wiki**](https://void.tarragon.be) to learn about the world and character creation.

---

## What is *The Void*?
"The Void" is a shared universe that supports both **D&D 5e** and **Pathfinder 2e**. 

Unlike a traditional campaign with the same 4 players every week, The Void has dozens of players and multiple GMs (Voidmasters). You create a character, and whenever a Voidmaster posts a session that fits your schedule, you sign up! 

Your character earns XP, gold, and reputation that carries over to every future session you play in that system.

## New to TTRPGs?
You are absolutely welcome! Most of our players started with D&D, but many have discovered a love for Pathfinder 2e's tactical depth. Our community is incredibly helpful—just mention you're new in Discord, and we'll help you build your first hero, regardless of which system you choose.

## How it Works
* **Communication**: Everything happens on our [Discord](https://discord.com/invite/TjDUu2Gkag). Assign yourself the "Void" role to see the channels.
* **Cost**: Sessions are **completely free** to attend. 
* **Support your Voidmaster**: While sessions are free, we highly encourage "tipping" your Voidmaster! A compliment, a cold drink, or a small donation is a great way to show your appreciation for the adventure they've prepared.
* **In-Person**: Most sessions take place at our venue in Kortrijk.

---

## Become a Voidmaster
Love to GM? We’re always looking for new Voidmasters! You have the freedom to run stories in the system you're most comfortable with.

* **Progression**: You earn XP and rewards for your own player characters for every session you host.
* **Collaboration**: Join our GM-only channels to help shape the world's evolving story.
* **Support**: We provide the framework, the wiki, and the planning tools; you provide the adventure.

Interested? Chat with us in the Discord or send a PM to one of the organizers!
